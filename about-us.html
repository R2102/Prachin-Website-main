Why Choose Us?

Setting the Standard in Pharmaceutical Excipients

With over three decades of experience, Prachin Pharmachem is a trusted manufacturing partner to pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and food companies worldwide. Our products consistently meet global pharmacopeial standards including IP, BP, USP, Ph.Eur., and JP, and are backed by certifications such as GMP, ISO, HALAL, KOSHER, Excipact, and USDMF. We pride ourselves on delivering reliable, high-performance excipients and ingredients with consistent quality and regulatory compliance. Supported by robust manufacturing, global distribution, and formulation-focused service, we enable our clients to develop with confidence and scale with certainty.
